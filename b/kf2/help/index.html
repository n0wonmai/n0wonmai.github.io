Server Commands

Type these commands in the KF2 server chat

!myid Shows your Player ID
!lk Shows your personal player information
!store Opens the Orgelbox store
!skins Shows all skins you own
!weapons Shows all weapons you own
!equip <id> Equip a weapon from your list
!skin <id> Apply a skin to your weapon
!clean Remove the skin from your weapon
!buy Open the donation shop page
!help Opens this help page

💡 Tip: Use !skins to see available skin IDs, then !skin <id> to apply them!
